Alphabet and pronunciations

The national language of Ethiopia is Amharic which is the second most spoken Semetic language worldwide and has its origin in the Amhara region. It is written with a version of Ge`ze script known as Fidel, which is a syllabary. There are 33 basic Fidels which are being altered in various ways by attaching a vowel.
