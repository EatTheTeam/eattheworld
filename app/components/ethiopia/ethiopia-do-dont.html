Invitation and payment

 It is expected to politely decline an invitation multiple times before accepting on the second or third time. The invitation shows hospitality and the refusal shows humbleness and that one is not greedy. That means that Ethiopians may refuse your offer out of politeness. Therefore, offer it more than once to give them the opportunity to accept something on the second or third attempt. It is inappropriate to ask to split a bill. The person who invites the other person to a meal is expected to pay at the restaurant.

Time

 punctuality is not that important. the pace of life is generally slower tasks may take a while to complete in Ethiopia, so plan more time and be patient if things last longer than expected.
